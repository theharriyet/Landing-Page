About Politocal Palette

Do you have problem detecting political stances behind the news you read everyday? Sick and tired of this kind of situation? Well, here is a Goole Extension that may easily solve your problem! With just one click on the small icon on the upper right of Chrome, Politocal Palette will quickly use AI techniques such as "key term analysis" and "sentiment analysis" to help you understand the stances of one article.
